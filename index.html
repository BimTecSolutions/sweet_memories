⚙️ Setup Guide

  1. Create a free account at cloudinary.com
  2. In your dashboard, find your Cloud Name
  3. Go to Settings → Upload → Add Upload Preset (set to Unsigned)
  4. Open config.js in this project
  5. Replace YOUR_CLOUD_NAME and YOUR_UPLOAD_PRESET with your values
  6. Save, push to GitHub, and you're live! 🎉
